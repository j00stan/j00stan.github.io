Self-Destruct Symposium
Posters for Event
Fall 2021

Self-Destruct is a symposium located within the Museum of the City of New York that discusses the barbaric and backwards military strategy mutually assured destruction. The goal of the project was to create 3 triptych's, 9 posters, discussing and advertising the event. Each triptych must visually look like a set, while being able to individually tell a variant of the entire story.
