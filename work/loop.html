LOOP*
Website
Spring 2022

LOOP* is a digital archive housing several interviews conducted during May of 2022. I asked those who I interviewed to get deep and personal about the rings they wear around their fingers. The website brings together several videos, images, audio recordings and transcripts of the interviews.

LOOP* Website

Ring Culture
Research Books
Spring 2022

The idea for LOOP* stemmed from the creation of 3 cultural research books on rings. Each book covers a different, unexpected aspect of ring culture, and each utilizie a different mode of design expression to display its contents.
