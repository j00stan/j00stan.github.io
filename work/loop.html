Project #3

LOOP*

Archival Website

Spring 2022

LOOP* is a digital archive housing several interviews conducted during May of 2022. I asked those who I interviewed to get deep and personal about the rings they wear around their fingers. The website brings together several videos, images, audio recordings and transcripts of the interviews.
