Project #2

MALA Typeface

Typeface

Spring 2022

MALA is a display typeface inspired by the Reggaeton artist, Bad Bunny. The typeface features thick stems, asymmetrically balanced letterforms, and is able to be used to form eye-catching headlines and beautiful typographic compositions. A vinyl cover and animation were created to best depict the typefaces qualities.
