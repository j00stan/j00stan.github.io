(1)Fashion Institute of Technology

A dynamic and fresh rebrand for the Fashion Institute of Technology. The "stretching i" motif showcases the school's limitless potential for individual creativity. Multiple touchpoints were created, alongside a brand guideline book to bolster the new brand image. All designs were created in collaboration with Molly Davis and Lauren Salerno.
