Ralph's Italian Ices
Rebranding
Spring 2021

Ralph's Italian Ices has a deep and rich history within New York culture, becoming a staple ice parlor in the state. The goal of the rebrand was to retain part of the brand's heritage while modernizing it for a younger future. Multiple touchpoints were created to showcase the new visual language of the brand, ranging from packaging, merch, signage and more.
