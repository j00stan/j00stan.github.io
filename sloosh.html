Sloosh Skincare
Branding & Packaging
Spring 2021

Sloosh is a skincare brand that targets young Chinese women, utilizing Ginseng, a Chinese root plant, as the main active ingredient. Ginseng is often referred to as the "infinity remedy" due to it's wide range of uses in Chinese medicine. The visual language behind the brand supports this idea while maintaining a minimal look.
