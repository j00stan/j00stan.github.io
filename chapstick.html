ChapStick
Advertising Campaign
Fall 2020

An advertising campaign that markets ChapStick as a Gen Z brand. A minimal design, with "in your face" copy and graphics, produced the best way to market Chapstick lip balm to a more socially concious generation. The poster series cross out what is unwanted in one's life, while putting foward one's true desires. Concept and design made in collaboration with Annalisa Zwirba.
