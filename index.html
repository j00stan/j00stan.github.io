About Me

Hi! My name is Justin.

Growing up, I was called "bubble boy" because of my long list of allergies. Thankfully, I've grown out of MOST of them. (Don't feed me eggs or shellfish.) What I haven't grown out of is my passion to always learn something new.

When I was 13, I built my first computer, piece by piece. From then on, I was introduced to the world of design, surprisingly through making Minecraft YouTube banners. (Ask me and I will show you some.) Looking back, I made some really cringy stuff, but I am grateful to see how much I have grown as a person, as well as a designer.

If you are interested in working together, shoot me a DM. (I promise I don't make cringy stuff anymore.)
